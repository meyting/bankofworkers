Task:

In this part of the task you have to solve 50 mathematical questions. In each of these you have to calculate the sum of 5 integer numbers between 1 and 10. You have 5 minutes to complete alle the 50 calculations. The task ends either if your time is over or if you have answered all the 50 questions.
[Keep in mind, that the answer will always be a positive integer. Thus, there won`t be any negative results or decimals. ???]
If you want, you can skip a question by clicking on "next". In that case you will be directed to the next question.
Please note that you cannot go back to the previous question after you have started with the next question by clicking "next".
Instead of clicking on "next" you can also press "enter" to go to the next question and to confirm your input. (This might save you some time.)


Example:


Adding the numbers gives the result 15. Therefore, the correct answer to enter is "15".


Score and payment:

For each correct answer, you get one point. You do not get any deductions for wrong or missing answers. Hence, your total score will be between 0 and 50, depending on how many math problems you have solved correctly.
(As explained previously, the bonus payment for this subtask is calculated by your score divided by 50?)


Example:


If you answer with the correct result "15", you get 1 point for that question.
If you answer a total of 30 out of 50 questions correctly, your overall score for this subtask will be 30.
(The bonus payment you get - if this task is drawn as the task that determines you extra money for part I - is 30/50 = 0.6$.)
