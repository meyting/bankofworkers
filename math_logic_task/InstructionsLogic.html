Now it is your turn to complete the sequences of numbers, i.e. to actually perform the task mentioned before.

You have {{C.time_sequence_mins}} minutes to complete a maximum of {{C.num_rounds_task}} sequences. In order to find the missing number of a sequence, you need to figure out the pattern according to which the number sequence is constructed. The task ends either after {{C.time_sequence_mins}} minutes or after you have completed all {{C.num_rounds_task}} sequences.

Example:

9 , 8 , 7 , 6 ,  

In this example, the rule according to which the sequence of numbers is constructed is to subtract 1 from the previous number in the sequence. Therefore, the correct answer would be "5".

You can always skip a sequence by clicking on "Next". In that case you will be directed to the next sequence. Please note that you can never go back to a previous question. Instead of clicking "Next" you can also press "enter" to submit your answer and move on to the next sequence.

Possible Bonus Payment:

For each correct answer, you get one point. You do not get any deductions for wrong or missing answers. Hence, your total score will be between 0 and {{C.num_rounds_task}}, depending on how many sequences you complete correctly. For each point, you earn {{ C.bonusrate }}.

Bonus Payment Example:

If you complete a total of {{C.bonusexample_sequence}} out of {{C.num_rounds_task}} sequences correctly, your overall score for this task will be {{C.bonusexample_sequence}}. The according bonus payment that you would receive if this task was drawn as the "task-that-counts" is {{C.bonusexample_sequence}} * {{C.bonusrate}} = {{C.bonusexampleresult_sequence}}.

If you have read and understood these explanations, please click "Next" to go to the next page to start the sequence task.
