The previous part is completed by several other people besides you. On the next two pages, we want to know what you think how well specific subgroups of people perform in the two tasks. Each subgroup consist of {{C.samples}} people.

More specifically, we ask you to predict...

  • ... the average score of all {{C.samples}} people of a given subgroup,
  • ... the average score of the top 10 people, i.e. those with the highest scores, of the respective subgroup,
  • ... the average score of the bottom 10 people, i.e. those with the lowest scores, of the respective subgroup.
